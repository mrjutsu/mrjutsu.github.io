I've been working with Git since I started learning Ruby and Rails, it's a software that makes my life easier when I need to save my progress and manage versions of my projects in order.

ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm David Rosillo, Multimedia Engineer and Web Developer. I was born in Barranquilla, Colombia in 1991. Since I was a child I was always interested in technology, software and video games, I was always in the computer, learning how the OS worked and how to customize it as much as I could. Back then I felt like an expert when I managed to fix something that didn't work.

After school I studied Multimedia Engineering in Cali, a warm and happy city in Colombia. At the university I learned how to code with languages like Java, ActionScript and MATLAB. However, it was after I graduated that I joined a bootcamp in Bogotá, there I learned Ruby and Rails. It changed my entire life.
